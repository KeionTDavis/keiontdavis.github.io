A Mental Health Resource

This was coded in  a few weeks or so to apply HTML, CSS and JS practically and develop out a responsive layout. I wanted to utilize calming colors that contrast the nature of mental illness, and chose a typeface with prominent ink traps to represent the holes and emptiness that mental illness can leave. Keeping the website simple and easy to navigate was vital as I this is to be an accessible source of information. ⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀ ⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀ ⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀⠀
